# Team Images

## Purpose
Photos of staff members, caregivers, and leadership team.

## Specifications
- **Dimensions:** 1200x800px
- **Aspect Ratio:** 3:2
- **Format:** JPG or WebP
- **File Size:** Max 300KB
- **Color Space:** sRGB

## Content Guidelines
- Professional headshots or full-body portraits
- Consistent background style across team photos
- Professional attire or scrubs/uniforms
- High-quality lighting and composition
- Friendly, approachable expressions
- Can include individual or group shots

## Naming Convention
- `team-{name}-{role}.{ext}`
- Examples:
  - `team-jane-doe-director.jpg`
  - `team-john-smith-rn.jpg`
  - `team-group-nursing-staff.jpg`

## Recommended Sections
- Leadership team
- Nursing staff
- Caregivers
- Administrative team
- Medical professionals (if applicable)

## Best Practices
- Use consistent lighting and background
- Maintain professional appearance
- Update photos regularly (every 2-3 years)
- Include diverse representation
- Obtain signed photo releases from all staff
