# Location Images

## Purpose
Banner images for service area locations and office facilities.

## Specifications
- **Dimensions:** 1920x600px
- **Aspect Ratio:** 16:5 (banner style)
- **Format:** JPG or WebP
- **File Size:** Max 400KB
- **Color Space:** sRGB

## Content Guidelines
- Wide banner-style images of:
  - Office exteriors/interiors
  - City skylines or landmarks
  - Local community scenes
  - Service area maps or regions
- Professional, welcoming appearance
- Good lighting and weather conditions
- Clear, sharp focus

## Naming Convention
- `location-{city/area}-{variant}.{ext}`
- Examples:
  - `location-downtown-office.jpg`
  - `location-west-side-skyline.jpg`
  - `location-north-county-map.jpg`
  - `location-facility-interior.jpg`

## Use Cases
- Service area pages
- Location-specific landing pages
- Contact page backgrounds
- Office location showcases

## Composition Tips
- Leave space for text overlays in center or sides
- Ensure important elements aren't cut off at mobile breakpoints
- Use subtle gradients or overlays for text readability
- Consider panoramic shots for wide coverage
