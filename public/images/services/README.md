# Service Images

## Purpose
Images representing individual home care services offered.

## Specifications
- **Dimensions:** 800x600px
- **Aspect Ratio:** 4:3
- **Format:** JPG or WebP
- **File Size:** Max 250KB
- **Color Space:** sRGB

## Content Guidelines
- Clear representation of each service type
- Show caregivers performing specific tasks:
  - Personal care assistance
  - Medication management
  - Meal preparation
  - Companion care
  - Physical therapy support
  - Housekeeping assistance
- Professional, welcoming atmosphere
- Diverse representation of patients and caregivers

## Naming Convention
- `service-{service-name}.{ext}`
- Examples:
  - `service-personal-care.jpg`
  - `service-medication-management.jpg`
  - `service-meal-preparation.jpg`
  - `service-companion-care.jpg`
  - `service-physical-therapy.jpg`
  - `service-housekeeping.jpg`

## Required Images
At minimum, one image per service type listed on the website.
