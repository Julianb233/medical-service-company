# Hero Images

## Purpose
Hero/banner images for the homepage and main landing sections.

## Specifications
- **Dimensions:** 1920x1080px
- **Aspect Ratio:** 16:9
- **Format:** JPG or WebP (for performance)
- **File Size:** Max 500KB (optimize for web)
- **Color Space:** sRGB

## Content Guidelines
- High-quality, professional photography
- Show caregivers and patients in positive, caring environments
- Should convey warmth, trust, and professionalism
- Ensure proper lighting and focus
- Avoid generic stock photos when possible

## Naming Convention
- `hero-{section}-{variant}.{ext}`
- Example: `hero-home-primary.jpg`, `hero-about-secondary.webp`

## Responsive Considerations
- Ensure important subjects are centered (mobile safe zones)
- Test on various screen sizes
- Consider providing multiple sizes for different breakpoints
